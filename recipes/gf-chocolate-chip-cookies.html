Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookies

A stock photo of chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredient List:

  • 270g King Arthur Cup for Cup Flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 180g brown sugar
  • 120g granulated sugar
  • 120g browned butter, room temp.
  • 2 eggs, room temp.
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients together with a fork.
  2. Cream suger and butter together until fluffy and lightened in color.
  3. Add egg one at a time, mixing between additions thouroughly.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredient mixture.
  5. Mix in chocolate chip.
  6. Put in airtight container and chill for at least 24 hours.
  7. Portion into 4 tsp. balls while chilled.
  8. Bake from chilled at 375 degrees for 11 min, slam on counter; 2 min, slam on counter; then bake 1 final min.
