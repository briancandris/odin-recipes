Grilled Cheese

This is a stock photo of a grilled cheese.

Ingredient List:

  • Sliced Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Kerrygold Butter
  • Olive Oil

Directions:

  1. Heat up butter and some olive oil in a stainless steel pan over medium heat.
  2. Place bread in pan once butter is melted and turn heat to low.
  3. Defrost bread by covering skillet with top.
  4. When bread is defrosted, add cheese.
  5. Cook until cheese is melted and combine halves.
  6. Sprinkle with salt to finish.
