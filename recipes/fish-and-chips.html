Fish and Chips

A stock picture of fish and chips.

Ingredient List:

Note: this recipe is intended for 1-1.5 lbs of cod.

  • Olive oil
  • 1.5 lbs of cod
  • 1.25 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of Kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1 can of good beer (12oz)

Directions:

  1. Put 2 quarts of peanut oil in a large pot and heat to 350 degrees.
  2. Make the beer barer in a mixing bowl, add all dry stuff, slowly mix in beer, then add the egg. Whisk until thouroughly combined.
  3. Slice the fish, filets or sticks. Make sure they are evenly shaped when dropping in the oil.
  4. Season the fish with salt for a few minutes then brush it off. Toss the fish in a bowl with flour that is seasoned with salt, pepper, and a bit of paprika. Coat all crevices and shake off excess. Dump the fish into the batter. Drop the fish in the oil facing away fro you. Cook for about 7 minutes or until golden brown. Repeat with the rest of the fish.
  5. Pull the fish out of the oil and let it rest on a wire rack, salt while its hot.
